1. To add a new migration to project use:

	Add-Migration -project:AHM.DataLayer

2. To update DB and apply all pending migrations use:

	Update-Database -project:AHM.DataLayer

3. To Rollback to specific migration use:
	
	Update-Database -project:AHM.DataLayer -TargetMigration:"MigrationName"
	
	NOTE: MigrationName is name without autogenerated prefix. Example: migration '201311251403411_TermUpdated' has name "TermUpdated"

4. To Rollback all migrations use:

	Update-Database -project:AHM.DataLayer -TargetMigration:0
